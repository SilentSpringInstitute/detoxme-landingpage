“A Clever Way To Avoid Toxic Chemicals In Everyday Products.”

The most reliable clean lifestyle guide that empowers you to eliminate toxic chemicals from your daily life.

This free smartphone app walks you through simple, research-based tips on how to reduce your exposure to potentially harmful chemicals where you live and work—and it keeps track of your progress.

We believe knowledge is not just power, but a prescription for prevention.

Track Your Progress

Take control of toxic chemicals with a personalized guide—learn about chemicals in products, explore ways to avoid them, track your progress, get reminders, and celebrate milestones! Each simple tip explains how making small changes can lead to better health.
